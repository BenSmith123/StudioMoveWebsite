Uniform

All Studio Move students are required to be in correct uniform for every lesson. These items, sourced from PW Dancewear and AS Colour, can be ordered in class or via email at any time.


    Baby ballet/Preschool dance
  • Leotard, skirt, ballet shoes (any colour). All uniform items available to order in class. Students in these classes are allowed to wear their own dancewear if approved by the studio. Shoes compulsory.


    • Junior-senior ballet
  • Navy leotard, wrap skirt, pink ballet tights and shoes.


    • Junior-intermediate jazz
  • Navy leotard, black dance shorts, black jazz shoes.


    • Senior-advanced jazz
  • Studio Move singlet, black dance shorts, jazz shoes.


    • Junior-intermediate contemporary
  • Navy leotard, black ¾ dance leggings.


    • Senior-advanced contemporary
  • Studio Move singlet, black ¾ dance leggings.


    • Hip hop
  • Studio Move t-shirt, grey shorts, white street shoes.


    • These Hips Hop too/Hip hop for everyone
  • These Hips Hop too t-shirt (available in 20 colours).


    • Gymnastics & Tumbling/Dance fit & stretch
  • Studio Move crop top, triple strap singlet, black dance shorts.


    • Optional items
  • Studio Move full length legging
  • Cross back crop top (blue)
  • Muscle back crop top (black)
  • Studio Move t-shirt (limited edition)
  • Studio Move Jacket (grey)
