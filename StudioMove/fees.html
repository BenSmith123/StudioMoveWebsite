Fees

The term fees for all classes are based upon a 35 week dance year. The term dates follow the New Zealand primary school terms, with some terms being longer than others. All class fees, with the exception of These Hips Hop too, are charged per term at the following rates.

  • Private lesson $25/half hour
  • Baby ballet/Preschool dance $70/term
  • Dance fit + stretch $75/term
  • 30 minute class (jazz, ballet, contemporary, tap) $80/term
  • 45 minute class (jazz, ballet, contemporary) $95/term
  • 60 minute class (jazz, ballet contemporary, tumbling) $105/term
  • Senior performance group $120/term
