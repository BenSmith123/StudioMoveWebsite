Studio Move

Studio Move offers classes for all ages and abilities, from toddlers, kids and teens to the over 60’s. Our students learn in a fun, challenging and uplifting environment and have many opportunities to perform throughout the year. The classes available to our students include jazz, ballet, contemporary, lyrical, hip hop, tap, tumbling, dance fit, stretch, competition solos, duos, troupes and more!

Studio Move is proud to be based in West Auckland at a variety of great locations. We facilitate the internationally renowned Asia Pacific Dance Association (APDA) and Hip Operation Dance Academy™ syllabi. Get in contact today, we would love to be a part of your dance journey!
